# 3. Recap

By adding these functions to your existing index.js file, you now have a full workflow:

* Supply WETH as collateral.
* Borrow USDC.
* Check your borrow balance.
* Partially or fully repay your debt.
* Withdraw your collateral.

This makes your code a one-stop shop for all the basic Comet interactions on Sepolia. From here, you can adapt or extend the script for your specific DeFi application.
